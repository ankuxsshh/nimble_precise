Nimble Precise was created by Anita Tom, Founder and Educator with over 30 years of teaching experience and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by working parents As the Founder and Educator of Nimble Precise.

Anita Tom understands the importance of Empowering Parents to take an active role in their child's education. With a passion for education and a commitment to supporting working parents, Anita Tom leads the Nimble Precise team in providing high-quality resources and support to families around the world.

Empowering working parents to thrive in their careers and nurture strong, loving relationships with their children.

To provide innovative solutions, expert guidance, and a supportive community that helps working parents achieve a better balance between their professional and family lives.
